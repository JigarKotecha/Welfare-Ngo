A CARING & LOVING HOME

There are millions of children in India without a family and a safe home. They are alone, invisible and emotionally vulnerable. We at Welfare Home for Children believe that each child deserves the love of a family and a protected environment to thrive. We are committed to care, protection, education and welfare of abandoned and destitute children
